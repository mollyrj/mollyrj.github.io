Hello! I'm Molly


I am currently studying "Art and Design" I am inspired by natural forms found in nature and the body. Recently I have been exploring the medium of watercolour and calligraphy. I make art because I have a burning passion to create beautiful things. I love illustration and feminism. I am growing and developing as an artist as I learn new mediums in my course and in my free time.

I also love to write and journal, turning my thoughts and ideas into artforms as a form of therapy to process various things. Here you will see my process thus far and I will be creating much more, you can stay updated with my new creations on my instagram account that will be linked in my contact section.
